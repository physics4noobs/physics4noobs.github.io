Quick Reference Notes

Chapter-wise formula sheets and key concepts for rapid revision. Everything you need at your fingertips.

🏁 Kinematics

Equations of motion for uniform acceleration, projectile motion formulas, and relative velocity essentials. Master the building blocks of mechanics.

v = u + at
s = ut + ½at²
v² = u² + 2as
Range = u²sin2θ / g

⚖ Newton's Laws of Motion

Force, friction, and circular motion fundamentals. Covers all three laws, free body diagrams, pseudo forces, and banking of roads.

F = ma
f = μN
ac = v² / r
Pseudo force = −ma

⚡ Work, Power & Energy

Work-energy theorem, conservation of energy, power, and collisions. Key formulas for kinetic energy, potential energy, and work done by variable forces.

W = F · d · cosθ
KE = ½mv²
PE = mgh
P = W / t

🔄 Rotational Motion

Torque, angular momentum, moment of inertia, and rolling motion. Covers parallel and perpendicular axis theorems and rotational kinetic energy.

τ = Iα
L = Iω
I = mr²
KErot = ½Iω²

🌊 Fluid Dynamics

Pressure, Bernoulli's principle, equation of continuity, and viscosity. Essential formulas for fluid statics and dynamics problems.

P = ρgh
A₁v₁ = A₂v₂
P + ½ρv² + ρgh = const

🔥 Heat & Thermodynamics

Gas laws, calorimetry, first and second laws of thermodynamics. Covers isothermal, adiabatic, isobaric, and isochoric processes with Carnot efficiency.

PV = nRT
Q = mcΔT
ΔU = Q − W
Efficiency = 1 − T₂ / T₁

〰 SHM & Waves

Simple harmonic motion, spring-mass systems, simple pendulum, and wave propagation. Covers displacement equations, time period, and wave velocity.

x = A sin(ωt + φ)
T = 2π√(m/k)
v = fλ
Tpendulum = 2π√(l/g)

⚡ Electrostatics

Coulomb's law, electric field and potential, Gauss's law, and capacitance. Core formulas for point charges, conductors, and parallel plate capacitors.

F = kq₁q₂ / r²
E = kq / r²
V = kq / r
C = εA / d

🔌 Current Electricity

Ohm's law, resistivity, power dissipation, and Kirchhoff's laws. Covers series and parallel combinations, Wheatstone bridge, and meter bridge.

V = IR
R = ρl / A
P = VI = I²R
Kirchhoff’s: ΣI = 0, ΣV = 0

🧲 Magnetism

Lorentz force, Biot-Savart law, Ampere's law, and cyclotron motion. Key formulas for force on moving charges and current-carrying conductors in magnetic fields.

F = qvB sinθ
B = μ₀I / 2πr
F = BIl
r = mv / qB

♻ Electromagnetic Induction

Faraday's law, Lenz's law, self and mutual inductance, and motional EMF. Essential formulas for flux, induced EMF, and energy stored in inductors.

ε = −dΦ / dt
Φ = BA cosθ
L = NΦ / I
ε = BLv

~~ Alternating Current

AC waveforms, phasor diagrams, impedance, and resonance. Covers RLC circuits, reactance, power factor, and resonant frequency.

V = V₀ sinωt
XL = ωL
XC = 1 / ωC
f₀ = 1 / 2π√(LC)

📡 Electromagnetic Waves

Maxwell's equations summary, speed of light derivation, and the electromagnetic spectrum. Key relations between electric and magnetic field components.

c = 1 / √(μ₀ε₀)
c = fλ
E / B = c

🔍 Ray Optics

Mirror and lens formula, magnification, Snell's law, and lensmaker's equation. Covers reflection, refraction, total internal reflection, and prism dispersion.

1/f = 1/v − 1/u
m = v / u
n₁ sinθ₁ = n₂ sinθ₂
1/f = (n−1)(1/R₁ − 1/R₂)

🌈 Wave Optics

Young's double slit experiment, fringe width, diffraction, and polarisation. Key formulas for interference patterns and Brewster's angle.

y = nλD / d
Fringe width β = λD / d
Brewster’s angle: tanθp = n

⚛ Modern Physics

Photoelectric effect, de Broglie wavelength, Bohr model, and nuclear physics. Covers photon energy, work function, hydrogen energy levels, and matter waves.

E = hf
KEmax = hf − φ
En = −13.6 / n² eV
λ = h / mv
