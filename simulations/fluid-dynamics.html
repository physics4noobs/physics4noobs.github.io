🌊 Bernoulli's Principle

Grade XI • Fluid Dynamics

About This Simulation

This simulation demonstrates Bernoulli's principle and the continuity equation. As fluid flows from a wider section to a narrower section of a pipe, the velocity increases and the pressure decreases. Watch the particles speed up in the constriction and observe the pressure gauges change accordingly.

A₁v₁ = A₂v₂   |   P + ½ρv² + ρgh = constant
