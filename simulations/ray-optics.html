🔍 Lens Ray Tracing

Grade XII • Ray Optics

About This Simulation

Interactive ray tracing through a convex or concave lens. Move the object distance and focal length to see how the image position, size, and nature (real/virtual, inverted/erect) change. Three principal rays are drawn: parallel ray, central ray, and focal ray.

1/v - 1/u = 1/f   |   m = v/u = h'/h   |   Power P = 1/f (in metres)
