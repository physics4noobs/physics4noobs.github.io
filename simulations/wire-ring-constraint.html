🔘 Bead on a Wire

Grade XI - Newton's Laws of Motion

About This Simulation

A bead slides frictionlessly on a circular wire under gravity. The wire exerts a normal (constraint) force on the bead, keeping it on the circular path. Watch how the bead oscillates and observe the constraint force direction.
