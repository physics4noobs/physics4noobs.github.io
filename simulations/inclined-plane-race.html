About This Simulation

Different objects rolling down an incline have different accelerations based on their moment of inertia. Objects with smaller moment of inertia coefficient (c) reach the bottom first.

a = g sinθ/(1+c)  |  c = I/MR²  |  Solid sphere: c=2/5, wins!
