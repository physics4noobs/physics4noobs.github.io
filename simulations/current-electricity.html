🔌 Simple Circuit

Grade XII • Current Electricity

About This Simulation

This interactive circuit simulator shows a simple series circuit with a battery, connecting wires, and a resistor. Animated electron dots flow through the circuit at a speed proportional to the current. Adjust the voltage and resistance to see how Ohm's Law governs the relationship between voltage, current, and resistance.
