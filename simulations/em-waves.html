📡 Electromagnetic Wave

Grade XII • Electromagnetic Waves

About This Simulation

Visualize an electromagnetic wave propagating through space. The electric field (E, cyan) oscillates vertically while the magnetic field (B, green) oscillates horizontally — both perpendicular to the direction of propagation and to each other. This is the fundamental nature of light and all electromagnetic radiation.
