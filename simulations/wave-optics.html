🌈 Young's Double Slit

Grade XII • Wave Optics

About This Simulation

Young's Double Slit experiment demonstrates the wave nature of light through interference. Coherent light passing through two narrow slits creates an interference pattern of bright and dark fringes on a distant screen. Adjust the slit separation, wavelength, and screen distance to see how the fringe pattern changes.

Fringe width β = λD/d   |   Bright fringe: y = nλD/d   |   Dark fringe: y = (2n+1)λD/2d
