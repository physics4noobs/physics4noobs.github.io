About This Simulation

An Atwood machine with a massive pulley accounts for the pulley's rotational inertia. The tensions on both sides are unequal, and the acceleration is reduced compared to a massless pulley.

a = (m₁-m₂)g/(m₁+m₂+M/2)  |  T₁ = m₁(g-a)  |  T₂ = m₂(g+a)
