⬜ Poisson's Ratio

Grade XI - Mechanical Properties of Solids

About This Simulation

Poisson's ratio describes how materials contract laterally when stretched. Most materials have positive ν (they thin when stretched), but auxetic materials expand laterally.

Key Formulas:
ν = -ε_lateral/ε_longitudinal
Range: -1 ≤ ν ≤ 0.5
Positive ν: material thins when stretched
Negative ν: material expands laterally when stretched
