🏁 Projectile: Angle Comparison

Grade XI • Kinematics

About This Simulation

Compare projectile trajectories at different launch angles. Notice that 45° gives maximum range and complementary angles (adding to 90°) produce equal ranges. Five projectiles are launched simultaneously to visualize these principles.

Range = v² sin(2θ) / g   |   Complementary angles give equal range
