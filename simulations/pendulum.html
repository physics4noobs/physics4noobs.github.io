About This Simulation

This simulation demonstrates a simple pendulum undergoing simple harmonic motion. The pendulum consists of a mass (bob) suspended from a fixed point by a massless string. When displaced from equilibrium and released, the pendulum oscillates with a period that depends on its length and the acceleration due to gravity. The simulation uses the actual pendulum equation (not small angle approximation) for accurate physics.
