⤴ Hooke's Law

Grade XI - Mechanical Properties of Solids

k: 50 N/m
Mass: 1.0 kg
Force F: 9.8 N
Extension x: 0.196 m
PE: 0.961 J

About This Simulation

Hooke's Law states that the force needed to extend a spring is proportional to the extension, within the elastic limit. Adjust the mass to see how the spring stretches and observe the linear relationship.

F = kx  |  x = mg/k  |  PE = ½kx²  |  k = F/x
