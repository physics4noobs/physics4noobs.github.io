🌍 Variation of Gravity

Grade XI - Gravitation

r/R: 1.00
g: 9.80 m/s²
Location: Surface
g₀ = 9.80 m/s²

About This Simulation

See how gravitational acceleration varies as you move from the center of the Earth to far above its surface. Inside the Earth, g increases linearly with distance from center. Outside, it follows an inverse-square law.

Inside: g = g₀(r/R)  |  Surface: g = g₀  |  Outside: g = g₀(R/r)²
