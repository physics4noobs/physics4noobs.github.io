⚖ Force & Acceleration

Grade XI • Newton's Laws of Motion

About This Simulation

Apply a horizontal force to a block on a surface. Adjust the applied force, mass, and coefficient of friction to observe Newton's Second Law (F=ma) in action. The block only accelerates when the net force exceeds static friction.

F_net = F_applied - f_friction   |   a = F_net / m   |   f = μmg
