Recommended Books

Curated book list for JEE, NEET, and Board preparation

THEORY For Concepts & Theory

📖

Concepts of Physics Vol. 1 & 2

H.C. Verma

The bible for physics concepts. Perfect for building strong fundamentals for JEE and NEET. Covers mechanics, waves, optics, and modern physics with excellent problems.

📚

Understanding Physics Series

D.C. Pandey (Arihant)

Comprehensive series covering Mechanics, Electricity & Magnetism, Optics & Modern Physics, and Waves & Thermodynamics. Great for JEE Advanced preparation.

📘

Fundamentals of Physics

Halliday, Resnick & Walker

International gold standard for physics. Excellent conceptual clarity with real-world applications. Recommended for deeper understanding.

📗

NCERT Physics Part I & II (Class XI & XII)

NCERT

Essential foundation. Every JEE and NEET question has roots in NCERT. Must-read before any reference book.

PROBLEMS For Problem Solving

📕

Problems in General Physics

I.E. Irodov

The ultimate challenge book. Legendary collection of problems for JEE Advanced aspirants seeking top ranks.

📒

Physics Galaxy Series

Ashish Arora

Modern approach with video solutions. Covers all topics with graded difficulty levels. Great for self-study.

📔

Cengage Physics Series

B.M. Sharma

Detailed theory with extensive problem sets. Excellent for JEE Main and Advanced with topic-wise practice.

NEET For NEET Specific

📙

Objective Physics

D.C. Pandey

Focused on NEET pattern with MCQs and previous year questions. Perfect balance of theory and practice.

📓

MTG Objective NCERT at your Fingertips

MTG Editorial Board

NCERT-based MCQs essential for NEET. Quick revision and comprehensive practice.

REFERENCE Reference Material

📜

VMC Physics Study Material (Class 11 & 12)

Vidyamandir Classes

Comprehensive modules with workbooks and solutions. Used as primary reference in this course. Covers all JEE topics with DTS (Daily Test Series) practice.
