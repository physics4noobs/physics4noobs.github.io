Who created Physics for Noobs?

Sankaracharya Dutta (Axomiya-Engineer) is a dedicated Physics educator whose journey began in the vibrant lands of Assam — Axomiya meaning “from Assam.” An IIT Guwahati graduate (B.Tech in Civil Engineering, 2014-18) with a remarkable CBSE score of 96% in PCM, he chose to follow his true passion: making Physics come alive for students.

With over a decade of teaching experience, Shankar Sir has mastered the art of transforming the “otherwise bland study of mathematical physics” into a spectacle of energy and attention capture. His unique teaching methodology combines flawless board work, stunning digital animations created with Photoshop and After Effects, and an infectious enthusiasm that makes every concept click.

Whether you’re targeting Class 12 boards or competitive exams like JEE and NEET, his organized and tailored approach to learning ensures every student gets personal attention.
